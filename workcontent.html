Physics Pirates.

2D Game made in OpenGL for Physics Module.

  • Solo

  • August 2022

  • C++ / Visual Studio

  • Source code currently hidden.

I made a physics-based game on the OpenGL engine based on a Pirate theme, complete with collisions, knockback, and boundary checking.

In it, you can break large rocks to obtain 3 different types of powerups - A Buddy Parrot, Missiles, and a Shield. There are also enemy pirate ships patrolling around the area, upon entering its range it will start chasing the player, while still keeping its distance.

After defeating 5 of them, a boss battle would begin.

The boss battle contains 3 phases, each introducing new mechanics that stack on top of each other.

The first phase would just be the boss shooting homing missiles at the player. Then, the second phase would cause the boss to start spawning an indicator at the player's current position, after a couple seconds, a cannon ball would be shot at the indicator and cause massive damage if the player has not moved away. The third phase would cause the boss to spawn minions that "kamikaze" towards the player.
